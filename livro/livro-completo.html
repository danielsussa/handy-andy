ABOUT HANDY ANDY

The kid who reads engineering books

Andy isn't a mechanic. He's a kid who likes engineering books.

Every night before bed, he opens one and finds a machine he's never seen before. He likes the cutaway drawings best — the ones that show you what's inside. Where the fire happens. Why the wheels turn.

This book is his collection. Every machine he's met so far, in the order he met them. From the first steam engines to the bullet train that hadn't even been built yet when his first book was printed.
